To declare variables as unsigned, use the syntax

unsigned X; /* or */
unsigned int X;

unsigned short Y;
unsigned long Z;

Each of the above have their smallest number as zero.
Unsigned is for non negative integers. The largest numbers are 2^n - 1, 2^16 - 1 = 64K - 1, and 2^32 - 1 = 4 gig - 1. %u is used for the unsigned data specification.
