Description Description

Symbols of belonging, military challenge coins show off your membership in an elite group - and no group is admired as the United States Marine Corps. Now, celebrate an honored tradition with the USMC Official Commemorative Challenge Coin Collection, the first-ever officially licensed and fully authorized by the United States Marine Corps coin collection, available ONLY from The Bradford Exchange. Highlighting the history, traditions and values all Marines share, your exciting USMC challenge coin collection begins with Issue One, Iwo Jima. Soon, your collection continues with Issue Two, Devil Dog, followed by Issue Three, 1st Marine Division and Issue Four, a custom glass-covered display case - a $100 value, yours for the same low price as a single issue. Additional USMC collectible coins, each a separate issue, will follow. Each issue will arrive separately.‡

Crafted of real metal with an impressive 2-inch diameter, each challenge commemorative coin in this collection is enhanced with hand-applied decorative enameling and 24K gold-plating to highlight the captivating details, and arrives sealed in a removable crystal-clear capsule to protect their superb numismatic quality. The impressive display case in a sleek black finish holds the collection on a background of dress blue trimmed with a red stripe, complete with a glass lid and a brass title plaque. Plus, the FREE informational booklet outlines the challenge coins and their significance in Marine Corps history and includes official images and facts about the United States Marine Corps. Strong demand is anticipated, and the edition is strictly limited to only 5,000 collections, so don't delay. Order now!
