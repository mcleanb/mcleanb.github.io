Statesboro Pairs
is a duplicate bridge club.



Our President is Chuck Johnson. He can be reached at 489-3921.

We are attempting to play a match event
this Tuesday. There will be two teams with 4 pairs each.
A third table is also playing regular BBO.

We are meeting as usual, on-line, at 7:00 pm, Tuesday. Start your table for 2 or 4 players,
entering everyone's BBO login names. We are playing 18 boards.
If you need help, call Chuck or Bruce. Call Bruce with your IMP scores at 678-6344.

After the virus, we will meet in the Episcopal Church on Veterans Parkway in Statesboro, Georgia.

The McLean's are inviting you to join them (virtually) at 3:00 Sunday for
a bridge game of 12 boards. Feel free to forward this to anyone that is already comfortable with BBO.

    Here are the rules for this Sunday. All participants can help change them later.
  • Register your pair before Sunday by email.
  • At 2:30 pm Sunday, the host will pull the pair names out of a hat.
  • The first 2 will be table 1, the second 2 will be table 2. etc.
  • If it is not an even number, the last pair plays against the world. From BBO competitive, select 'I have a partner'.
  • Each pair will keep track of their total IMP score. If the 2 IMP scores
    are different, take the average. Winners of the IMP scoring will be announced and posted on the web site. No additional prizes should be expected.
  • Using facetime and/or texting is encouraged. This is party bridge.

    If you like bridge, you'll love duplicate. Come join us.
