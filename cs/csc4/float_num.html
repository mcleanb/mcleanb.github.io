A floating point number contains a real decimal point, not just an implied one. Examples are float (%f, %E, or %e), and double (%f, %E, or %e).

%f12.123456 6 digit default
%10.6f12.1234567 just fits
%5.2f12.12just fits
%10.2f12.12 right justified
%3.2f12.12 left justified
%e1.23456e+01default
%10.2e__1.23e+01 field width = 10
 int (%d), short (%hd), and long (%ld) are not floating point numbers.
